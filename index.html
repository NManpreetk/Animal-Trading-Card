Dog

The domestic dog is a member of the genus Canis, which forms part of the wolf-like canids, and is the most widely abundant terrestrial carnivore.

  • Family: Canidae
  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Gestation period: 58 – 68 days
  • Class: Mammalia

Today, dogs are such familiar parts of our lives—our reputed best friends and subject of many a meme—that it’s easy to take them, and what they represent, for granted.
